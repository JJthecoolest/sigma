What Are Unblocked Games?

Unblocked games are online games that can be played without restrictions, even on networks where gaming sites are usually blocked. These games are often accessible through proxy servers, special websites, or platforms that bypass school or workplace firewalls.

They are popular among students and employees looking for a quick entertainment break. Since they don't require downloads or installations, unblocked games provide instant fun without violating network policies.
