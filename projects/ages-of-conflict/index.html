Ages of Conflict

Welcome to Ages of Conflict

Ages of Conflict is an innovative and dynamic nation simulation game where players can witness the rise and fall of civilizations across a procedurally generated map. The game simulates historical, fictional, and completely randomized scenarios where AI-controlled nations battle for survival, dominance, and expansion. Whether you're watching mighty empires grow or tiny regions suddenly conquer the world, Ages of Conflict offers an unpredictable, fascinating spectacle.

This game is not about direct control—it's about observation, tweaking parameters, and letting simulated intelligence determine the course of history. With each run, a new story unfolds. You'll see peaceful alliances, brutal takeovers, betrayals, sudden downfalls, and glorious victories. The world map evolves in real-time, and it's up to you to sit back and enjoy the chaos or interfere slightly with settings to see what happens.

Ages of Conflict was developed using Unity WebGL, making it accessible directly in your browser without any installations. It supports detailed visualization, high performance, and a seamless experience across different systems—though WebGL builds are not supported on mobile devices due to limitations in performance and input methods.

Features of the Game

  • Simulated warfare between nations on a dynamic map.
  • Custom maps, editable nation settings, and randomness modifiers.
  • AI-driven diplomacy, war declarations, and population growth.
  • Color-coded regions for easy tracking of expansions and collapses.
  • Multiple save slots and export options for screenshots and videos.

Whether you're a history lover, simulation enthusiast, or enjoy watching emergent gameplay, Ages of Conflict delivers an engaging and often surprising visual experience. The best part is that every simulation is different—no two outcomes are ever alike.

With regular updates and an active community, new features and improvements are consistently being added to keep the experience fresh. You can even share your own maps or download those created by others to spice up the action.

Who Should Play?

This game is perfect for:

  • Fans of simulation games and sandbox environments.
  • Content creators and streamers looking for unique visuals.
  • Gamers interested in world-building and political evolution.
  • Students or educators who want to explore geopolitical mechanics in a fun way.

No two games are ever the same in Ages of Conflict. The procedural generation and AI interactions make sure each playthrough offers a fresh challenge. It's not about "winning" but about watching events unfold and seeing how tiny sparks ignite great wars—or how massive empires crumble from within.

Start the simulation, sit back, and let history write itself before your eyes.
